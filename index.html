About Cinnamon Secrets

Welcome to Cinnamon Secrets!

Hi sweet friends! I'm Alice Meyer, the baker behind all the cozy goodness here at Cinnamon Secrets. What started as a little hobby has grown into a true passion—and I'm so excited to finally share it with you!

I've been baking since I was a kid, but over the last 4 years, cinnamon rolls have completely stolen my heart. Each batch is homemade, filled with love (and lots of cinnamon), and made right here in Huntley, IL.

Whether you're here for a classic cinnamon roll, something sweet and new, or just want to follow along for all the deliciousness, I'm so happy you're here. Your support means the world, and I can't wait to share this journey with you!

Stay tuned into our socials (linked at the bottom) for menu updates, order info, and mouthwatering photos you won't want to scroll past!

With love & sugar,
Alice
